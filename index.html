a raving world
about
a raving world is an exporlation into a posthuman cartography, incorporating poststructural geophilosophies to represent the fluid boundaries and relations between the people, technology, and spaces that consitute rave culture.

Each radial gradient represents a rave as a plateau in a process of becoming. Click on a rave to enter rave space.

Click on "a raving world" to return to the default view.
